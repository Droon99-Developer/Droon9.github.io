The Afterschool Campaign

War Sweeps Ionia, as the War of Life rages. The Stoneskull Aliiance's army stands, newly trained, with Nostok Ironchest at it's helm. The Elven nation of Borin has retracted into the Faewild, just as the Lightleaf clan formally joins the Scaleborn Hirearchy on the battlefield agains the Briarheart and Oakpond Armies. The Leviathan Dynasty has lost control of their military, as their General, Adrian Flosian prepares an assault on Leviathanian, his own capital. The Elven lands are in squabbles as well, and the world is in a grim place. Midpoint, the bastion of the former Tetra-alliance, is now a neutral zone for those who wish for a night of rest in this land of chaos and lawlessness.
