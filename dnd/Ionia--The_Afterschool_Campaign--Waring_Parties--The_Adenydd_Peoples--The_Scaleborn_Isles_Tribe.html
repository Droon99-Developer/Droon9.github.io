Johansare
Dagger, Legendary (Requires Attunement)

Johansare switches between 2 states every 1d4+3 rounds (rolled at the time of a switch). Roll a d2 to decide between Corruption and Destruction.

Johan, Dagger of Corruption
This sickly green dagger spew poison, diesiese, and curses from its heart.
Upon a sucessful hit, roll a d6 to determine the effect of the blade
On a 1-2, The attacked creature must make a dc 19 constitution save or take 12d6 poison. Save for half
On a 3-4, The attacked creature takes an extra 2d6 poison damage, and have to make a dc 15 con save or be affected by Sewer Rot (DMG. 157)
On a 5, The attacked creature must make a Wisdom saving throw at the start of each of its turns. lf it fails, it wastes its action that turn doing nothing. The curse can only be removed by a greater restoration of 6th level or higher.
On a 6, The attacked creature gains one of the previous effects, and gains a point of Corruption (as seen on the Shadowfell Despair Table) until dispelled by a Wish or a curse removal spell at 8th level or higher. (the Dread effect is replaced with Bloodlust from the Abyss Table, and the Mad Ambition and Demonic Possesion Traits are added to the end of the table.
